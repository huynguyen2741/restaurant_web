VIET CHOPSTICKS RESTAURANT
Leesburg & Woodbridge
'The Masters of Herbs and Spices'


'Traditional Vietnamese cooking is greatly admired for freshness of the ingredients and for the healthy eating style.'
Our cuisine features a combination of five fundamental taste elements in the overall meal: spicy (Metal), sour (Wood), bitter (Fire), salty (Water) and sweet (Eath). Each of our dishes has a distinctive flavor which reflects one or more of this elements.
Our Chefs are the Masters of making the balance between fresh herbs, meats, seafood, vegetables and a selective use of spices to reach a fine taste
Please also visit our other locations in Woodbridge and Springfield if you are in the areas. Viet Chopsticks Woodbridge and Saigon Quan Restaurant.
