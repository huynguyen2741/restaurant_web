When you are in the mood for good times and great cuisine, be sure to check out our Viet Chopsticks Restaurant. The restaurant is located in one of the area's most pleasant settings, the Lansdowne Town Center, and is known for its delightful staff and superb cuisine. We have our full bar with wide selections of mix drinks, wine and beer, check out our Vietnamese beer: 'Saigon' and '33' beer. Also, you and your love ones can enjoy your meal outdoor on our patio on a nice beautiful day. The menu at Viet Chopsticks Restaurant features a wide array of great selections, make from only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, with something sure to please every member of your group. We also serve many vegetarian dishes, vegetarian noodle soup and fresh vegetarian garden rolls. Viet Chopsticks Restaurant has established itself as one of the area's favorite culinary destinations and is sure to offer you a pleasant and unique dinning experience every time you visit.
